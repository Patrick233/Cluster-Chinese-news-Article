Versatileversatile /ˈvɜ：sətaɪl/adj。 多才多艺的；多功能的It means that someone has many different skills， or something can be used for many different purposes。它指的是某人掌握多种技能或某物有多种用途。He is very versatile as a famous writer and actor。他很多才多艺，既是著名的作家又是演员。This machine is so versatile and practical。这个机器多功能并且很实用。来源：每日学英语微信公众账号
